The Focused MVP

One canal. One bottleneck.
One billion-dollar decision.

The project pivots from broad polar routing to the world's most commercially sensitive waterway. Panama is a sharper MVP: drought compresses draft, queue auctions distort voyage economics, and every transit decision becomes a tradeoff between time, fuel, insurance and customer penalty.

NSR-Aegis converts climate and oceanographic telemetry into an operational recommendation — accept Panama transit, reduce draft and wait, bid for priority, or trigger tactical diversion via the Strait of Magellan.
